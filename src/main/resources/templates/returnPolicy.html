Return & Refund:

Every product is expected to arrive at your doorstep in great condition.However there may be instance of transit stress which may result in arrival of product in unexpected condition.

Ensure packing is not tampered before providing acknowledgement to courier. Acknowledged courier document will imply packing was intact during receipt.

If you are not happy with your purchase; wrong or damaged product is delivered, then it has to be informed to us with images within 2 days from date of delivery. We will accept returns and exchanges on unused or unopened products within 7 days of purchase. Refund procedure is commenced only after receipt of product by supplier.

If you dislike the product received, you can follow below steps to send the product to us:

  1. You can return the item to us within seven days from the delivery date.
  2. We will send a replacement or initiate a refund for the product to your source account once we receive the product.
  3. You will bear the shipping charges to return the product.

Opened parcel will require supplier inspection before decision to replace on return or refund or reject. The supplier will have to be convinced for the reason of return. We will initiate a refund or send the replacement product accordingly. Supplier must receive the product duly packed, back for inspection to make decision for replacement or refund.

The return address will be that of the supplier and advisable to use the same courier. Food items with short expiry and items marked non-refundable will not be accepted for refund.

The Refund amount will be paid by Somodesk within 2 days upon supplier`s decision to refund only after receipt of returned product. Deductions to recover cost of execution will apply. The decision of Somodesk with respect to Return and Refund will be final and binding on our customers.

The Refund amount will be credited back by Somodesk vide same tender as per the mode of payment receipt, after deduction of transaction cost, for cases, wherein somodesk or supplier is not at fault.

Simply reach out to our customer support team at info@somodesk.com to start the process.
