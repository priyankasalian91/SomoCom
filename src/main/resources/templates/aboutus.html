About Somodesk


Somodesk is an affiliate B2B2C network to sell and buy products online.

Somodesk offers you much more than an e-commerce business experience. Just like an e-commerce shop, the site somodesk.com, which you’re currently accessing, offers various products and services online. It helps you browse products and services, add them to your cart and buy them.

The prime objective of e-commerce is to empower entrepreneurship and help grow businesses across the country. It is about helping budding and established businesses to improve their sales and profits. Somodesk wants to help passionate business persons promote, sell their products.

Somodesk wants to create a community to help the businesses engage themselves in complimentary services so that they all can grow together.

Somodesk.in offers these opportunities through Somodesk.com

Somodesk.in registers Affiliates who promote our Alliance Partners’ products and services. Alliance Partners are the suppliers of products and services. Affiliates resolve the most important challenges that our Alliance Partners face - marketing, promotion that leads to sales. This very expensive necessity is effortlessly handled by our Affiliates.

You can continue to buy a regular valuable customer at somodesk.com, or you can signup to login as an Affiliate at somodesk.in to earn, learn and grow. You, as our valued Customer, Affiliate or Alliance Partner together help us build a vibrant ecosystem to benefit all.

Somodesk is the only ecommerce portal empowering women, the challenged and the seniors to fulfill their dreams of entrepreneurship and earn to lead a dignified life.
