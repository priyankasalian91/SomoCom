What is the execution time?

All the orders are dispatched by the supplier within 2 working days or as per the delivery date mentioned by you at the time of placing the order. Most of the orders are delivered to your address within 4 - 5 working days from the date when the order is placed. For remote locations orders will be delivered by India Post within 6 - 8 working days. Delivery will be duly done as per your specified time and date.

However, few products could be substantial and require more time for the delivery. In such a case, we will notify you at the earliest. Our only aim is to keep you happy and fulfill your requirement at the shortest period.

Can I track my order?

Yes, we have an easy-to-use tracking system for our customers who can track the order by logging into the My Account section of our website. Once the order is shipped to your destination, we send you a tracking ID that you can use to check your shipment status from our website.

Do you cater to Bulk orders?

Yes, Somodesk is a B2C and B2B portal wherein customers are invited by a large complement of Affiliates who are the sales force on our site. Visitors are required to register to buy. Customers are provided an option to purchase low quantities with payment. Registered Visitors / Customers can lodge an enquiry for bulk requirement of desired products. and somodesk staff will assist you for the purchase. Somodesk will also cater to project sales for enquiries received on site.

We usually process bulk or wholesale orders through personalised service through our parent company Albertsons International Pvt. Ltd., which has a legacy of existence for over 25 years through a network of branch offices pan-India.
